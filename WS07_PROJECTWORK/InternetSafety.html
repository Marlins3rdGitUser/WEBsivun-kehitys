Internet Safety

Question: Whats better than having a good anti-virus?
Awnser: Not getting any malware at all!

Four steps for safe internet browsing

Use a safe web browser

Good web browsers have systems or features that make browsing the internet not only safer, but also makes it look cleaner with features such as ad-blocking.

Avoid suspicious websites

Most of the time, sketchy/suspicious websites have some malicious features that can cause harm just by simply visiting the website, such as trying to install malware without the needing user interaciton.

Verify that the website is the real one

Check the URL link for the website. Sometimes other websites try to imitate the real website to try to get information, such as login details for the real website or try to make you download malware.

Know what you are clicking before you click

It is a good idea to know what you are about click before clicking. Sometimes when clicking something, it can cause you to get sent to another website or download something you didn't want.
